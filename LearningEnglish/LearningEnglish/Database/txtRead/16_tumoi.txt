<b>Approval</b>: official permission</br>
<b>Party</b>: an organization of people with particular political beliefs in local or national government</br>
<b>Argue</b>: to speak angrily to someone, telling them that you disagree with them</br>
<b>Pretend</b>: to behave as if something is true when you know that it is not</br>
<b>Armed</b>: using or carrying weapons</br>
<b>Injured</b>: hurt or physically harmed</br>
<b>Prevent</b>: to stop something from happening or someone from doing something</br>
<b>Victim</b>: someone or something which has been hurt, damaged or killed or has suffered</br>
