<b>Custom</b>: a way of behaving or a belief which has been established for a long time</br>
<b>Arrange</b>: to plan or prepare for; to organize</br>
<b>Merchant</b>: a person whose job is to buy and sell products in large amounts, especially by trading with other countries</br>
<b>Sign</b>: a notice giving information, directions, a warning, etc</br>
<b>Security</b>: when something is not likely to fail or be lost</br>
<b>Establish</b>: to start a company or organization that will continue for a long time</br>
<b>Conservative</b>: tending not to like or trust change, especially sudden change</br>
<b>common-law</b>: someone who is not officially a wife or husband but is considered to be one because she or he has been living with their partner for a long time</br>
<b>legalize</b>: to allow something by law</br>
